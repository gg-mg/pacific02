The Yurts at Pacific Trails Resort

What is a yurt?
Our luxurt yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground.Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gased-fired stove. The luxury camping expeience also includes electricity and a sink with hot
and cold running water.Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should i bring?
Bring sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.
